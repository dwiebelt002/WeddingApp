How the Prince Met a Princess

Rachel and Devin’s story began in May 2015 in Orlando, Florida. They met thanks to the modern convenience of technology on a popular dating site known as Match.com. Through the sea of endless profiles, searching and waiting for the right one to come along, they finally ended up in each others lives after finding each other and taking a leap of faith to meet one another. It was truly an instant connection as their first date was only at the most magical and happiest place on earth, Walt Disney World. With so much to talk about and sharing so much curiosity and fascination with one another one date led to another and that connection grew everyday after. There was so much to learn about one another as Devin learned about China and the culture as well as some basic Mandarin phrases, including the bad words that he shouldn’t learn (bad Devin!) Rachel learned about everything Devin knew about American Pop Culture and indoctrinated her into the Nerdy World of Star Wars, Star Trek, Marvel and DC Comics, and many other films and shows Rachel hadn’t really known too much about. Together they have shared in many adventures and continue to do so.


Some of the things Rachel and Devin love to do together is go to Disney and Universal, share thier love of food, and learn new things from each other. They seek to travel more in the future and see the world together. Most important of all, they bring out the best in each other, always pushing each other for success and finding the best in each other despite any faults or failures.


In April 2016, Devin asked Rachel to be his wife so that they can share their lives together. It was a happy day celebrated at the same place that brought them together, Disney. They now look forward to seeing you soon to celebrate this new chapter and beginning as they venture forward into Happily Ever After as Husband and Wife!
